About Coastal Peak Renovations

A home improvement company built on honest work, clear communication, and a genuine commitment to Virginia homeowners.

Our Story

Built for the Homes and Homeowners of Virginia

Coastal Peak Renovations was founded with one clear goal: to give Virginia homeowners access to reliable, high-quality home improvement work — without the frustration that too often comes with the contractor experience.

We serve two of Virginia's most active home markets — Hampton Roads and Greater Richmond. These are communities we know well, and the homes in them come with specific challenges: coastal humidity, aging systems, clay soils, and the year-round demands of the Virginia climate.

Our services span the full range of what homeowners need — from kitchen and bathroom remodels and custom tile work to deck building, framing, sheds and garages, fencing, siding, exterior doors and windows, flooring, home additions, and radon mitigation. Whatever the project, we bring the same standard of work and the same level of communication.

We show up on time. We communicate clearly. We do the work right. We stand behind the result. That's the formula — and we don't compromise on it.

Our Mission

Bringing Homes Back to Life — One Project at a Time

Every home has a story. Some need renewal — a kitchen brought into this decade, a bathroom that finally feels like a retreat, a backyard deck that becomes a gathering place.
