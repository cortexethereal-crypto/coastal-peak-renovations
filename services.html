Home Improvement Services for Hampton Roads and Greater Richmond

We handle the full range of remodeling and improvement projects that protect, upgrade, and add value to your home.

From a complete kitchen remodel to a new detached garage, custom tile work to radon mitigation — Coastal Peak Renovations is a one-stop home improvement team for Virginia homeowners. Every job gets a free estimate, clear communication, and quality work from start to finish.

Kitchen Remodeling

Your kitchen is the heart of your home — and one of the highest-ROI renovation investments you can make. We handle full kitchen remodels including cabinet replacement or refacing, countertop installation (granite, quartz, butcher block), tile backsplash, new flooring, layout changes, lighting upgrades, and appliance integration. We work with your vision and your budget to deliver a kitchen that functions beautifully and looks great.

Bathroom Remodeling

From a simple vanity upgrade to a full gut-and-rebuild, we handle bathroom remodels of every size. Our services include tile installation for floors and showers, vanity and fixture replacement, walk-in shower conversions, tub-to-shower conversions, behind-tile waterproofing, lighting upgrades, and full bathroom design. We work in primary bathrooms, guest baths, and half baths throughout the home.

Learn more about Bathroom Remodeling →

Custom Tile Work

Tile done right makes a dramatic difference. We install floor tile, wall tile, shower surrounds, kitchen and bathroom backsplashes, entryway tile, and outdoor tile surfaces. We work with ceramic, porcelain, natural stone, large-format slabs, and decorative mosaic tile. Whether you want a clean, simple layout or a custom herringbone or chevron pattern, we bring the precision the work requires.

Learn more about Custom Tile Work →

Deck Building

A well-built deck adds usable square footage, increases home value, and becomes a space your family actually uses. We build new decks from the ground up using pressure-treated lumber, composite decking (Trex, TimberTech), or hardwood — designed to handle Virginia's coastal and inland climate. We also handle deck repairs, board replacement, railing upgrades, staining and sealing, and both attached and freestanding structures.

Learn more about Deck Building →

Sheds & Garages

Need more storage, a workshop, or a dedicated garage space? We build custom sheds and detached garages from the ground up — handling everything from site preparation and concrete work to framing, siding, roofing, and doors. We can match your home's existing exterior style or build from a new design. All structures are built to local code and designed to last.

Fencing

A quality fence adds privacy, security, and definition to your property. We install privacy fencing, picket fencing, split-rail fencing, and decorative fencing in a range of materials including wood, vinyl, aluminum, and chain link. We handle post setting, panel installation, gates, and repairs — and we help you navigate HOA requirements and local permitting when needed.

We install and replace siding in vinyl, fiber cement (HardiePlank), and engineered wood — giving your home a fresh appearance while improving its durability, moisture resistance, and energy performance. We also handle soffit, fascia, gutters, and exterior trim as part of a complete exterior upgrade.

Radon Mitigation

Radon is a colorless, odorless gas that seeps into homes from the soil — and it's the second leading cause of lung cancer in the U.S. If your home tests at or above 4 pCi/L, mitigation is recommended by the EPA. We install sub-slab depressurization systems that safely vent radon from beneath your home to the outside, bringing levels down to safe ranges reliably and permanently.
