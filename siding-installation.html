Siding Installation

Exterior Protection and Curb Appeal That Lasts

Siding plays a major role in protecting the exterior of your home while also shaping curb appeal. Coastal Peak Renovations provides siding installation services to help homeowners across Hampton Roads and Greater Richmond improve both the protection and appearance of their homes.

When siding ages, weathers, or becomes damaged, it stops doing its job effectively. Moisture can work its way behind failing siding, finishes fade and crack, and what was once a clean exterior begins to show its years. Replacing siding with quality materials and professional installation restores that protection while giving the home a noticeably improved appearance.

We work with homeowners to select siding solutions that make sense for the home, the climate, and the project goals. Our focus is on dependable service, clean installation, and results that hold up over time without requiring constant maintenance.

If your home's siding is showing signs of wear, damage, or age-related decline, Coastal Peak Renovations can help you evaluate your options and move forward with a solution that protects and improves your home for years to come.

Common Homeowner Concerns

Signs Your Siding May Need Replacement

Aging or Damaged Siding

Cracked, warped, or physically damaged siding that no longer provides reliable protection for the home's exterior.

Fading or Weather Wear

Siding that has lost its color, finish, or protective properties due to prolonged sun, moisture, and weather exposure.

Exterior Moisture Concerns

Signs of moisture intrusion, rot, or water damage at the siding or in the materials behind it.

Curb Appeal Issues

Exterior appearance that no longer reflects the quality of the home or is affecting the overall look of the property.

Maintenance Headaches

Siding that requires frequent painting, caulking, or repair and is no longer worth the ongoing upkeep.

Preparing for a Future Sale

Siding condition is one of the first things buyers notice. Fresh, well-maintained siding signals a cared-for home and can help the property stand out during a future sale.

Siding replacement costs in Virginia typically range from $8,000 to $30,000 or more depending on the square footage of the home, material choice, and the condition of the existing sheathing underneath. Vinyl siding falls on the lower end, while fiber cement (HardiePlank) and engineered wood run toward the higher end and beyond. Homes with significant moisture damage, rotted sheathing, or extensive trim work add to the total. The best way to get an accurate number is a free exterior assessment and estimate — every home is different.
