Sheds & Garages

More Space on Your Property, Built the Right Way

A quality shed or detached garage adds real, usable space to your property — whether you need somewhere to store equipment, park vehicles, run a workshop, or simply get the clutter out of your home. Coastal Peak Renovations builds custom sheds and detached garages for homeowners across Hampton Roads and Greater Richmond who want a structure built right and built to last.

Prefabricated structures and big-box shed kits often look fine on day one and start showing their limitations within a few years. Settling, warping, and water intrusion are common in structures that were not built on solid footings with proper materials. We construct outbuildings the same way we approach any residential project — with attention to foundation, framing, weatherproofing, and finish.

Whether you need a compact storage shed for lawn equipment, a large workshop structure, or a detached two-car garage with a proper roof and doors, we work through the details with you from the start. That means understanding your goals, your property, and how the structure will be used day to day.

Coastal Peak Renovations serves homeowners across Hampton Roads and Greater Richmond for shed and garage builds of all sizes. If you are ready to add usable space to your property, request a free estimate and we will walk through the project together.

Common Homeowner Needs

Reasons Homeowners Add Sheds and Garages

Lack of Storage

Lawn equipment, tools, seasonal gear, and outdoor furniture need a dedicated home that is off the property and out of the way.

No Covered Vehicle Parking

A detached garage protects vehicles from the weather, reduces wear, and keeps the home's attached space available for other uses.

Workshop or Hobby Space

A dedicated outbuilding gives hobbyists, woodworkers, and DIY homeowners a proper workspace without taking over the garage or basement.

Replacing a Worn Structure

An old shed that is rotting, leaning, or no longer weathertight is often better replaced than repaired — and a new build can be sized and positioned exactly where you need it.

Property Value Improvement

A well-built garage or storage structure adds functional appeal and can improve how a property presents during a future sale.

Overflow from the Home

When closets, attics, and garages are full, an outbuilding creates breathing room for the household without requiring major interior renovations.
