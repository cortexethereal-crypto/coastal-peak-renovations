Deck Building

Outdoor Living Space Built to Last

A well-built deck adds outdoor living space, improves enjoyment of the property, and can increase the functionality of your home's exterior. Coastal Peak Renovations provides deck building services designed for durability, appearance, and long-term use across Hampton Roads and Greater Richmond.

Whether you are starting from scratch or replacing an aging structure, we help homeowners design and build decks that fit the home, support everyday use, and hold up through Virginia's seasons. Our approach focuses on strong workmanship and materials selected for the demands of outdoor living.

We work with homeowners to understand their goals — whether they want a simple rear deck for relaxing, a larger entertaining space, or something built to fit a specific part of the property. Every project begins with a free estimate and honest conversation about what will work best for the home.

Coastal Peak Renovations brings a practical design mindset to every deck project, with a focus on durable results and dependable service from first contact through final inspection.

Common Homeowner Concerns

Signs You May Be Ready for a New Deck

No Outdoor Living Space

Your backyard has potential that is not being used because there is no functional space to relax, entertain, or enjoy the outdoors.

Aging or Damaged Deck

An existing deck that is weathered, structurally compromised, or past its useful life and needs to be replaced.

Poor Layout

A deck that does not work the way it should for everyday use, entertaining, or the natural flow from the home's interior.

Worn Materials

Decking boards, railings, or structural components that have deteriorated and affect safety or appearance.

Limited Backyard Usability

Outdoor space that is underutilized because it lacks a functional, comfortable area for spending time outside.

Wanting Space for Outdoor Entertaining

A dedicated deck gives families a proper place for cookouts, gatherings, and outdoor activities that a bare yard simply cannot support as comfortably or consistently.

Deck construction in Virginia typically ranges from $12,000 to $60,000 or more depending on size, material choice, and features. A standard pressure-treated wood deck falls on the lower end, while a large composite deck with built-in lighting, a pergola, or multi-level design runs toward the higher end and beyond. Material alone — pressure-treated wood vs. composite vs. hardwood — can double the cost of the same footprint. The best way to get an accurate number is a free on-site estimate — every deck project is different.
Pressure-treated wood costs less upfront but requires regular sealing and maintenance. Composite decking costs more initially but is virtually maintenance-free, resists moisture and fading, and typically carries a 25-year warranty. We help you choose based on your budget and long-term goals.
